# Technology Decision Matrix Standard

## Protocol
1.  **Weight Assignment**: Assign a significance weight (1-5) to each criterion.
2.  **Scoring**: Evaluate each candidate technology on a scale of 1-5.
3.  **Calculation**: Compute the weighted product for each cell.

## Scoring Legend
*   **1 - Critical Deficiency**: Fundamental limitations that make the solution unviable.
*   **2 - Suboptimal**: Significant drawbacks or risks.
*   **3 - Adequate**: Meets the baseline requirements.
*   **4 - Superior**: Demonstrates strong alignment with requirements.
*   **5 - Exceptional**: Best-in-class capability.
